# Lyra — Manga Project

Type: Illustration / Storytelling / Worldbuilding  
Year: 2025  

## Description
A dystopian story following the last surviving human and two opposing AI systems.

## Context
Developed during my exchange semester in Japan as part of a university course focused on manga and narrative design.

## Focus
The project explores themes of coexistence, control, and the relationship between humans and artificial intelligence. The story centers on Lyra, a young girl navigating a world shaped by two conflicting AI entities — one designed to preserve life, the other to eliminate humanity.

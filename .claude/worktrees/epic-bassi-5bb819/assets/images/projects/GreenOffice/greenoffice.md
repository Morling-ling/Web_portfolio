# Green Office — Bookmarks & Stickers

Type: Illustration / Communication Design  
Year: 2026  

## Description
A series of illustrated bookmarks and stickers designed for sustainability communication.

## Context
Created as part of my work as a student assistant at the Green Office, TH Mannheim.

## Focus
The bookmark concept plays with visibility and hidden growth: a plant emerges above the book while its roots remain inside, suggesting that change begins beneath the surface. The designs aim to communicate sustainability in a subtle, approachable way.

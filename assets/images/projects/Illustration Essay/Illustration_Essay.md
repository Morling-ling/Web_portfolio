# Japan — Illustration Essay

Type: Illustration / Visual Essay  
Year: 2025  

## Description
A visual essay reflecting on everyday experiences during my exchange semester in Japan.

## Context
Created during my exchange semester in Japan as part of a university course. The project explores my perspective as a foreigner encountering a new culture.

## Focus
Observation of small, often overlooked details and moments that felt unique, unfamiliar, or meaningful. The work combines illustration and narrative to capture atmosphere, cultural nuances, and personal impressions.
